The Password Strength Meter

What is it?

There are many password strength meters in place today. You see them when you are registering for almot any website telling you if your password is 'weak', 'strong' or somewhere in between. But what does 'weak' or 'strong' really mean? With this thought in mind we decided to create a meter that tells you a little bit more about the passwords you create.



How does it work?

How it works is simple, you will be asked to provide a password, we will ask you some questions about the password and based on the password and your answers we will tell you about your password. If you are eager to try it out then go ahead and click on the button below to try it out.
